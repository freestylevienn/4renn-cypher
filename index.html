WHAT IS 4RENN CYPHER?

It's the first official Karatina University pre-recorded rap battle series, giving rappers a platform to shine. Each season features 10 talented MCs battling it out in a high-energy cypher format. The winner takes home KES 5,000 and bragging rights as the reigning champion.
